### Syllabus for Certificate Education

All lessons for the Certificate Education are provided using online multimedia facilities in an easy-to-learn and interesting manner. The details of these lessons are given below.

#### AM00 - Agaram (Equivalent to Grades 1 & 2)

This level is created to cover subject matters such as Introduction to Letters, Learning Words, Learning Small Phrases, Writing Practice, Nursery Rhymes, Moral Stories, and Simple Grammar.

The lessons are designed to provide training in listening, speaking, reading, and writing in Tamil. It covers not only relationship terms in Tamil but also the names of objects used in daily life and classrooms, as well as objects found around us such as trees, plants, creepers, flowers, vegetables, fruits, electrical appliances, and vehicles.

The lessons are created based on themes such as Language, Country, Society, Nature, Education, Industry, Art, Culture, Science, Technology, Ethics, Wellbeing, Personality, and Humanity. They incorporate prose sections and grammar focused on language elements like words, phrases, and meaning.

Furthermore, the lessons are designed to provide training in sentence construction, general knowledge tidbits, translation, and creativity.
