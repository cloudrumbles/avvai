திணை - The Five Landscapes

Our learning journey is inspired by the ancient Sangam concept of Thinai the five poetic landscapes that mapped human emotions to the natural world.

மருதம் Marutham - Paddy fields, new beginnings
பாலை Palai - Desert, endurance
நெய்தல் Neithal - Seashore, longing
முல்லை Mullai - Forest, patience
குறிஞ்சி Kurinji - Mountains, union
