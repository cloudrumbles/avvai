Grammar Notes

Formal vs Informal Address

Tamil distinguishes between formal and informal speech through verb endings and pronouns. When greeting elders or strangers, use the formal endings -ங்க (-nga).

எப்படி இருக்கீங்க? "How are you?" (formal/respectful)
எப்படி இருக்க? "How are you?" (informal/friendly)

Tip: When in doubt, use the formal form. It's always respectful and never offensive.

The Greeting Gesture

வணக்கம் is traditionally accompanied by joining both palms together at chest level, similar to the Indian namaste. This gesture is called கைகூப்புதல் (kai koopputhal).
